Current Opportunities

Accounting/Tax Intership

Required Skills & Abilities

- Accounting majors who intend to begin their careers in public accounting.
- Students in their junior or senior years, with at least one section of Intermediate accounting already successfully passed.
- GPA's of 3.0 and higher.
- Availability of 15-20 hours per week during spring semester is preferred
- Interns should be able to work in a fast-paced environment with deadlines, multi-task, and possess critical thinking skills.
- Interns should be aspiring to obtain CPA certification
- Interns should have a willingness and desire to learn more about public accounting.
- Attributes that demonstrate initiative, career-oriented intensity, above average Excel/IT skills, and professionalism will be     significant factors in our selection of candidates.
- Candidate must be bilingual ( English and Spanish ) 
- Excellent customer service and communication skills.
- Knowledge of MS Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint) and QuickBooks.

How To Apply

To apply for the accounting/Tax internship position, please email your resume and CV indicating why you would be a great fit to info@abcpatax.com.
